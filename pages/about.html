About Us

Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNV) Tudipaju Phulbani, situated amidst the serene landscapes of Phulbani, Odisha, stands as an esteemed educational institution committed to the provision of quality education to students hailing from economically disadvantaged rural backgrounds. Since its inception, JNV Tudipaju Phulbani has maintained a steadfast focus on holistic development, fostering not only academic excellence but also instilling values of character, discipline, and community service. The institution's contemporary infrastructure and a dedicated faculty complement its mission of creating an intellectually stimulating environment.

The alumni cohort of JNV Tudipaju Phulbani, particularly those who graduated during its formative years, exemplify the institution's commitment to nurturing well-rounded individuals. Many among the alumni have pursued advanced education at distinguished national and international institutions, underscoring the school's dedication to academic prowess. The impact of JNV Tudipaju Phulbani's educational ethos extends beyond academic accomplishments, as its alumni have made notable strides in professional spheres, community service endeavors, and entrepreneurial pursuits. Rooted in a shared sense of values and camaraderie cultivated during their time at the institution, the alumni actively engage in philanthropic initiatives, further exemplifying the enduring influence of JNV Tudipaju Phulbani on the trajectories of its graduates.
